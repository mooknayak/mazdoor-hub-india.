About Us

Mazdoor Hub India was started by Diwakar Kumar with a simple goal: make it easier for India's daily-wage and skilled workers — masons, plumbers, electricians, painters, carpenters, drivers, farm labor, and more — to be found by the people who need to hire them, without paying any agency or middleman.

Workers can register a profile in a few minutes using just their mobile number, add their skills, location, rate, and photos or videos of their past work. Employers can then search by state, district, category, or pincode to find a worker near them, and call directly.

Our Mission

To make it easier for India's workforce to find consistent work, and for employers to find reliable, local talent — using nothing more than a mobile phone.

How It Works

  • Workers register with their mobile number, verified via OTP.
  • They fill in their category, location, experience, and rate.
  • Employers search by state, district, category, and pincode.
  • Employers reveal the worker's number and call directly to discuss work.
